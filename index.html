Getting to Stockholm

Best airport to fly to is Stockholm Arlanda Airport (ARN) located about 35 minutes from central Stockholm.

Options from the airport are either Ubers/taxis or the Arlanda Express train which takes 20 minutes to Stockholm Centralstation.

Places to Stay

The coach will be picking up and dropping off at Stockholm Centralstation. There are plenty of hotels nearby but if you hate scrolling through Booking.com, we’ve listed a few nearby options here!

  • Scandic No 53 – The Premier Inn of Sweden
  • Elite Hotel Adlon – Slightly fancier if that’s your vibe
  • Radisson Blu Royal Viking Hotel – Got a pool and the word Viking in the name

Stockholm is a relatively small city with great public transport so feel free to venture further away and find a cooler hotel or AirBnB on your own!

When we stay in Stockholm we tend to spend most of our time in the area of Södermalm!
